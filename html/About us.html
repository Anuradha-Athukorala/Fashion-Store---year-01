About Us


Unique Fashion – Sri Lanka’s biggest fashion chain offers a wide range of clothes and accessories for Men, Women and Kids. The retail store houses a range of Homeware and Lifestyle products to compliment a comprehensive family shopping experience for all our customers. Our showrooms are located in strategic cities across the Island, in addition to our head office which is located in Colombo



Unique Fashion has been growing from strength to strength over the last 10 years with a team of 500 people serving millions of customers every year. The retail chain takes pride in its Sri Lankan roots but offers a world-class retail experience that drives customer loyalty while simultaneously setting new benchmarks in customer-centricity to take the art of retailing to a new level.
